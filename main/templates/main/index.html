About The Trust

The vision of our trust is to work with the marginalized and under – privileged rural populace; to pull it out of poverty and place it on a new platform of economic self-sufficiency, morality and prosperity. We aim to provide complete support, protection and care to needy and deprived people in the society with special focus on rural communities; and aim to bring in change in their lives.
